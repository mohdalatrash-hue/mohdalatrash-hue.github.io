8

~30 sec — brief, punchy takeaway
Total: ~10 min. Aim for 7–9 slides. Avoid reading from the slides — use them as visual anchors only.
Opening hook ideas — choose one for your presentation
Surprising statistic: "A 2021 survey found that 80% of university students in Saudi Arabia use social media for more than 4 hours per day — yet its effect on academic writing has received almost no attention in the literature."
Rhetorical question: "Have you ever spent more time revising a peer's essay than your own? If so, you may already know why giving feedback can sometimes teach more than receiving it."
Define the gap directly: "Despite decades of peer feedback research, almost no study has examined its effectiveness specifically among Saudi EFL learners at the undergraduate level. Our study begins to fill that gap."
During the 3-minute Q&A: Listen carefully, repeat or rephrase the question, answer directly (don't ramble), and if you don't know — say so honestly. Practise with these likely questions from your audience and Dr. Alatrash.
