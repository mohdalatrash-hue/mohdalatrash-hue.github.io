Resources & Downloads

Every template, guide, and worked sample you need for the group research project — in one place. All files open as PDFs.

📌 How to use these

Start from the Proposal Template and the Final Submission Guidelines — they define the structure your group's paper must follow. The guides explain how to do each stage; the samples show you what good looks like. Graded submission is always in Blackboard Ultra.
