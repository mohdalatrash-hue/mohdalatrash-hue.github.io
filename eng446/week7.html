Exam Preparation — What to Study

Final Exam · Aug 16–17 · Individual · Closed book · 50% of grade · Covers all chapters + APA 7
1
What is research? (Intro + Ch1)

Definition, types (qualitative, quantitative, mixed, basic, applied), characteristics (systematic, purposeful, rigorous, iterative)

2
Research paradigms & ethics (Ch2)

Positivism, interpretivism, critical paradigm · ontology, epistemology · informed consent, confidentiality, no harm

3
Research questions, aims, objectives (Ch3)

Distinction between aims, objectives, and research questions · scope and feasibility

4
Literature searching & source evaluation (Ch4)

Boolean operators, databases, CRAAP test, primary vs. secondary sources, peer review

5
APA 7 in-text citation (Ch5)

Author-date format, paraphrase vs. direct quote, one author vs. multiple authors vs. et al., page numbers

6
Literature review structure (Ch6)

Thematic, chronological, comparative · critical synthesis · identifying the research gap

7
APA 7 reference list (Ch7)

Journal articles, books, book chapters, websites · hanging indent, sentence case, italics, DOI

8
Research designs (Ch8)

Survey, interview, observation, case study, document analysis · matching design to question

9
Instrument design (Ch9)

Questionnaire design principles · Likert scale · open vs. closed questions · pilot study · interview schedule · probes

10
Qualitative analysis (Ch10)

Thematic analysis steps (familiarise, code, theme, review, define, write up) · coding · saturation

11
Quantitative analysis (Ch11)

Descriptive statistics: frequency, mean, mode, range · tables and charts · labelling figures

12
Writing up (Ch12 + Conclusion)

Report structure (abstract through references) · discussion vs. findings · limitations · recommendations · reflexivity
