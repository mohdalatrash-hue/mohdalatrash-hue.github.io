APA 7 In-Text Rule: Use author–date: (Last, Year) for a paraphrase, or (Last, Year, p. X) for a direct quote. Two authors: both names every time. Three or more: first author + et al. No author: use shortened title in quotes.
APA 7 Reference List Rules: Hanging indent · sentence case for titles · italicise journal name and volume · DOI as hyperlink · alphabetical by first author's surname · date in parentheses after author · no "Retrieved from" unless no DOI.
