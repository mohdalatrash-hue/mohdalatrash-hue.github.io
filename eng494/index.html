Literary Translation

A self-study studio for translating prose, children's literature, poetry and drama into Arabic · Landers (2001) · Dr. Muhammad Alatrash

Jun 28 – Aug 17, 2026 · Section 173 · Sun–Mon–Tue · 8:00–9:40 AM · Room 3151, College of Languages

🛠️ A studio you run yourself

Each workshop below teaches a translation skill, then asks you to translate a short real excerpt and self-assess against a model approach. Work at your own pace; your progress is saved on your device. Graded quizzes, projects and the final are in Blackboard Ultra.
