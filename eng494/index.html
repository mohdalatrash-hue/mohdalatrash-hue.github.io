What to expect

ENG 494 explores the art and craft of literary translation — the complex decisions involved in carrying meaning, tone, style, and cultural nuance from one language into another. The course examines theoretical frameworks alongside hands-on translation practice across three major genres.

Poetry translation Prose & fiction Drama & script Translation theory Cultural adaptation
